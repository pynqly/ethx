Privacy Policy for ETH Staking Optimizer (Freemium)

Last Updated: November 2025

1. Overview

The ETH Staking Optimizer Chrome Extension does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data. The extension operates fully on your device and only displays yield data from a public GitHub JSON file.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

  • No names
  • No email addresses
  • No wallet addresses
  • No browsing history
  • No device identifiers
  • No analytics or tracking of any kind

3. What Data the Extension Accesses

The extension only reads a single publicly hosted JSON file containing ETH yield data:

https://raw.githubusercontent.com/<your-username>/ethx/main/snapshot.json

No other network requests are made.

4. Use of Third-Party Services

The extension does not use:

  • Google Analytics
  • Fingerprinting scripts
  • Tracking cookies
  • Advertising networks

5. Local Storage (Optional)

If settings or display preferences are saved, they remain only on your device via Chrome’s local extension storage. No data ever leaves your device.

6. Security

Because no data is collected or transmitted, there is no user information at risk. All logic runs locally in the browser.
